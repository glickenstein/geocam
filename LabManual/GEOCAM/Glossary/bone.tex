bone.\qquad In a simplicial $n$-manifold, a bone generally refers to a
$\left(  n-2\right)  $-dimensional simplex, so in a $3$-manifold, bones
consist of (usually interior) edges. Sometimes bone refers to not only the
simplex, but also all of the $n$-simplices incident on the simplex. This
terminology is mainly used in the Regge calculus literature.
